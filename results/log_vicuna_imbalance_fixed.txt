DIAGNOSIS
==============================================================================

  • IMBALANCE_MATTERS: Balanced Vicuna 253+253 improves HPS TPR5 from 0.594 → 0.672 (Δ +0.077). Class imbalance is part of the issue.
  • LLAMA_ROBUST_TO_IMBALANCE: Llama-3 with Vicuna-style imbalance (416+253) achieves HPS TPR5 = 0.999. Imbalance alone does NOT break HPS — it's something Vicuna-specific.
  • PER_ATTACK_FAILURE: HPS specifically fails on attack 'gcg' (HPS rate 0.076, C4 rate 0.994). HPS may have an attack-specific blind spot.
  • MULTIPLE_HARD_ATTACKS: HPS is >0.2 worse than C4 on 6 attacks: ['autodan', 'gcg', 'ijp', 'pair', 'puzzler', 'zulu']
  • GAP_PERSISTS: Even with balancing and best layers, HPS only reaches 0.672, vs C4's 0.985. Some Vicuna-specific property continues to disadvantage HPS — investigation should focus on per-attack failures or mechanism beyond what we've tested.
